Crafted in the Heart of Nairobi

Azani was born from a passion for preserving and celebrating African textile traditions while creating contemporary fashion that speaks to the modern world. Our atelier in Nairobi combines generations of tailoring expertise with innovative design.

Every piece tells a story - from the vibrant kente cloth sourced from local artisans to the intricate beadwork that adorns our evening wear. We believe fashion should honor heritage while embracing the future.
